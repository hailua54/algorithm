                  E hiu phng phap s dung l2 norm nh th nay. Khi giai bai toan cc tiu hoa ham li f bng phng phap gradient descent chng han thi cng thc cp nht trong s la w' = w - alpha x f'(w). Khi thm l2 norm vao thi f'(w) se tr thanh f'(w) + w. T o, w' = w - alpha x (f'(w)+w) = w - alpha x f'(w) - alpha x w, nghia la dich chuyn gia tri cua w i 1 khoang alpha x w. Thi nu mang nron bi overfit tai w' ma gia tri ta nhn c lai la w' - alpha x w, nn ta mong i rng tai im w' - alpha x w se khng bi overfit.
